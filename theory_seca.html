Theory Problems - Secondary/JC/Poly/ITE Level

Note: This page contains sample multiple-choice questions designed for secondary school, junior college, polytechnic, and ITE students participating in the National Junior AI Olympiad.

Programming & AI Fundamentals

Sample questions will cover:

  • Advanced programming concepts and data structures
  • Machine learning algorithms and their applications
  • Computational complexity and optimization
  • AI problem-solving techniques

Deep Learning & Neural Networks

Sample questions will cover:

  • Neural network architectures (CNN, RNN, Transformers)
  • Backpropagation and gradient descent
  • Activation functions and loss functions
  • Overfitting, regularization, and model evaluation

Unsupervised Learning & AI Ethics

Sample questions will cover:

  • Clustering algorithms (K-means, hierarchical clustering)
  • Dimensionality reduction (PCA, t-SNE)
  • AI bias, fairness, and transparency
  • Privacy and data protection in AI systems

Generative AI

Sample questions will cover:

  • Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs)
  • Variational Autoencoders (VAEs)
  • Large Language Models and Transformers
  • Diffusion models and image generation

Real-world AI Applications

Sample questions will cover:

  • Computer vision and image processing
  • Natural language processing and sentiment analysis
  • Recommendation systems and collaborative filtering
  • AI in healthcare, finance, and autonomous systems
