Optimisation

Finding the Best Solutions to Problems

Overview

Optimization is the process of finding the best solution from a set of possible alternatives. In AI and machine learning, optimization algorithms are used to minimize loss functions and find optimal model parameters.

Key Topics

Gradient Descent

First-order iterative optimization algorithm.

Stochastic Optimization

Sampling-based approaches for large-scale problems.

Constrained Optimization

Optimization with constraints and boundaries.

Convex Optimization

Optimization of convex functions with guaranteed global optima.

Hyperparameter Tuning

Grid search, random search, and Bayesian optimization.

Multi-objective Optimization

Balancing multiple competing objectives.
